For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring freuently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: in a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and ewait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, piour liquids into the frozen basin and process accoprding to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thick for twnety minutes before adding mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.
